Create a single continuous hollow pipe shaped like a wide upright U.

The pipe should consist of a long horizontal lower section connected at both ends to two vertical upward sections through smooth 90-degree bends.

Use a uniform circular pipe profile with an outer diameter of approximately 20 mm and a wall thickness of approximately 2 mm.

Make the lower horizontal section approximately 140 mm long. Make each vertical section approximately 100 mm high. Use smooth bends with a centerline bend radius of approximately 25 mm.

The left and right vertical sections should be approximately parallel and similar in height. Both upper pipe ends must remain open and face upward, clearly showing the hollow interior and wall thickness.

The entire structure must form one connected watertight pipe body with no disconnected parts, unintended intersections, sharp corners, or blocked internal passages.
